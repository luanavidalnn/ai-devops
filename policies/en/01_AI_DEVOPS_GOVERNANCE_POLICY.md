# AI DevOps Governance Policy

## Objective
Define guardrails for safe and consistent use of AI in DevOps workflows.

## Principles
- Human-in-the-loop for operational decisions
- Evidence-first recommendations
- Least privilege access
- Auditability and traceability

## Allowed Use Cases
- Incident triage support
- CI/CD risk summarization
- Drafting operational documentation

## Restricted Use Cases
- Autonomous production changes without approval
- Secrets exposure in prompts or outputs
- Actions outside approved environments

## Mandatory Controls
- Approved prompt templates
- Human review checklist
- Incident and change logging
- Periodic policy compliance review

## Exceptions
Any exception must be approved by security and platform owners.
